- Flight info: A single longhaul hop, typically under eight hours, depending on winds and routing.
- JFK arrivals: Arrivals are well-signed. Expect immigration, baggage, then a choice of AirTrain connections, taxi, or rideshare into Manhattan.

## 3. WHY NEW YORK WORKS FOR A CITY BREAK (split layout)

New York is the rare city that suits both planners and play-it-by-ear types.

- Direct and straightforward: fly in, drop bags, start the day.
- Neighbourhood-led: each area feels like its own mini city.
- Iconic, without effort: the "seen it in films" moments are genuinely everywhere.
- Indoors or outdoors: museums, markets, parks, viewpoints, all year round.
- Brilliant in short bursts: you can stack experiences close together and never feel stuck.

## 6. BROADWAY (evergreen guidance module)

Broadway is less about "what's on" and more about doing the night well.

### How to do Broadway well

- Timing: aim to arrive early enough for a calm drink and a quick photo outside the theatre.
- Seating, at a high level: front sections feel immersive, mid-to-rear often give you the best overall view of staging. Side seats can be great value if you're happy with a slightly angled view.
- Matinees: ideal if you want an evening free for restaurants, skyline views, or a quieter night.

### Choose your style

- Big night-out spectacle: high energy, big staging, all the drama (the fun kind)
- Classic New York theatre: story-first, timeless, and properly New York
- Family-friendly matinee: earlier curtain, easier pacing for younger travellers

## 7. GETTING AROUND + JFK (two side-by-side cards)

### Card 1: Getting around NYC

Subway basics
Fast, frequent, and the easiest way to cover distance. Use contactless payment where available and follow station signage.

Walking
Often the quickest way between nearby neighbourhoods, plus it's how New York shows off.

Taxis and rideshare
Great late at night, in bad weather, or when you've done your steps for the day.

Accessibility note
Not every subway station has step-free access, so plan routes with accessibility in mind if you need it.

[Image: Couple walking through New York neighbourhood]

### Card 2: JFK to and from the city

AirTrain + subway
Best for value and predictability, especially at busier times.

Taxi
Best for simplicity, particularly with luggage or a group.

Rideshare
Best for flexibility, with pick-up instructions depending on terminal.

Timing
Allow a broad window, journey times can vary with traffic and time of day.
